🧮 Math

Learn about fractions, algebra, and geometry — the building blocks of secondary math!

  • What is algebra? (x + 2 = 5 → x = 3!)
  • Fun Fact: Zero was invented over 1,500 years ago!

🔬 Science

Explore chemistry, physics, and biology — discover how the world works!

  • Everything around you is made of atoms.
  • Plants make their own food using sunlight — that’s photosynthesis!

📚 English

Learn how to write essays, read stories, and use new vocabulary.

  • Tip: Every sentence needs a subject and a verb.
  • Fun Word: “Curious” means eager to learn!

🏺 History

Discover amazing stories from the past — ancient Egypt, explorers, and inventors!

  • The Great Pyramid is over 4,000 years old.
  • The first airplane flew in 1903 — that’s just over 100 years ago!
